# 🥛 Amul Protein Tracker Bot – Complete Guide

Welcome to the **Amul Protein Tracker Bot**! 🎉 
This bot is your personal, 24/7 scout that monitors the Amul online store and alerts you instantly when your favorite protein products are back in stock.

### 🌟 Why this tracker is built differently
Unlike standard basic stock checkers, this tool is packed with power-user features. It dynamically calculates the absolute **Best Value (Price/Gram)** so you never overpay, provides **Multi-Pincode Fallbacks** to check backup locations instantly, and guards your cart with dedicated **Freebie & New Product Alerts** unseen in other bots.

---

## ✨ Features & Functionality (In Detail)

### 1. 🎯 Keyword Tracking
Don't want to refresh the website all day? Just tell the bot what you want!
- **Specific Products:** Track exactly what you need (e.g., `Amul High Protein Blueberry Shake`).
- **Categories:** Track broad keywords (e.g., `Whey`, `Paneer`, `Shake`).
- **Speed:** The bot scans Amul's servers **continuously**.
- **Instant Alerts:** You will get a message the exact second your tracked item becomes available in your pincode.

### 2. 📍 Smart Multi-Pincode Fallback
Stock availability varies heavily depending on the city or warehouse. To counter this, the bot features an advanced location fallback system.
- **Primary Pincode:** Your main location (e.g., your home address).
- **Secondary Pincodes (Backups):** You can set up to 2 backup pincodes (e.g., your office, or your hometown).
- **How it works:** If you are tracking `Chocolate Shake` and it is *Sold Out* at your Primary Pincode, the bot will automatically dive in and check your Secondary Pincodes. If it finds stock there, it will send you a special "Secondary Pincode Alert"!

### 3. 🏆 Best Value Finder & USP Display
Tired of doing mental math to figure out which protein pack is the cheapest?
- **Unit Sale Price (USP):** Every time you check stock, the bot calculates the exact price per gram (for food) or per milliliter (for liquids).
- **The Crown 👑:** The bot automatically identifies the absolute best deal per category and highlights it with a 👑.

### 4. 🎁 Dedicated Promos, Freebies & Coupon Alerts
Items bundled with a **FREE SHAKER**, special "Gift Packs", or that have active **Coupons** are highly contested and sell out in minutes.
- The bot features a dedicated radar just for high-value promos and coupons.
- Easily toggle this radar ON or OFF using the `🎁 Freebie/Coupon` button on your menu.
- When enabled, the bot will notify you instantly if any promotional bundle or coupon code becomes available.

### 5. 🆕 Fresh Drops (New Product Launcher)
Amul frequently launches secret new flavors or products (like High Protein Wheat Flour or Greek Yogurt variants).
- When a brand-new, never-before-seen item appears on the store, the bot will broadcast an urgent **🆕 New Product Launch** alert to all users.

### 6. ⏰ Daily Briefing (Automated Daily Stock Check)
Stay passively informed without needing to click anything.
- Set a custom time of day (IST) using the `⏰ Set Time` menu.
- The bot will deliver a comprehensive, beautifully formatted layout of every protein product's availability in your primary location.
- Toggle this automation ON or OFF via the `🔔 Report` button.
